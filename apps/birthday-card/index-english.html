A Love Letter

Thirty-five springs,
and every one more beautiful
because of you.

I still remember the moment I first saw you — like the opening scene of the greatest love story ever written. It was the moment the word fate finally made sense to me.

Like cherry blossoms drifting over Seoul in spring, your smile colors every ordinary day into something luminous. No leading lady on any screen could ever outshine the woman standing before me.

I am so grateful that you were born into this world — that thirty-five years ago, someone as extraordinary as you came into existence, and somehow, your story and mine became one.

Every spring from now on, I want to spend at your side. I love you — today, tomorrow, and always.

Yours completely,

Terrance

L

To My
Beloved Wife

With all my heart —
Happy Birthday

You are the leading lady of my life
