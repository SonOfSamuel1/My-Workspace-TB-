# Reverting to Stock Firmware

The jailbreak is fully reversible. Follow these steps to restore the official
Eight Sleep firmware.

## Steps

1. **Remove pod from your Eight Sleep app account**
   - Open the Eight Sleep app
   - Go to Settings > Remove Pod
   - Confirm removal

2. **Reset firmware**
   - Follow the pod-specific reset procedure in the Free Sleep docs:
     <https://github.com/throwaway31265/free-sleep>
   - Pod 5 can be factory reset without risk of bricking

3. **Reconfigure as new pod**
   - Open the Eight Sleep app
   - Set up the pod as if it were brand new
   - Re-subscribe if desired

## Notes

- All Free Sleep data and configurations will be wiped
- Your Eight Sleep account history may not be recoverable
- The pod will resume requiring a subscription for full features
