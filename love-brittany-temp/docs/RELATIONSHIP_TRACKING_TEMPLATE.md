# Love Brittany Action Plan - Tracking Document Template

This document provides the template structure for your Google Doc tracking document. Copy this content into a new Google Doc to track your relationship activities.

## Instructions

1. **Create a new Google Doc**
2. **Copy the template below** into your document
3. **Get the Document ID** from the URL:
   - URL format: `https://docs.google.com/document/d/[DOCUMENT_ID]/edit`
   - Copy the `DOCUMENT_ID` part
4. **Add to your .env file**: `RELATIONSHIP_TRACKING_DOC_ID=your_document_id_here`

## Important Formatting Notes

- **Keep section headers exactly as shown** (e.g., `[GIFTS]`, `[LETTERS]`)
- **Use the checkbox format**: `‚ñ° Date: YYYY-MM-DD | Details`
- **Date format must be**: `YYYY-MM-DD` (e.g., 2025-10-24)
- **Mark completed with**: `‚òë` or `[x]` before the date
- **Keep the pipe separator** `|` between date and details

## Tips for Success

### Daily Practice
- Set a recurring 10-minute calendar reminder titled "Love Action - Brittany Time"
- Use this time to check gaps, plan actions, or reflect

### Weekly Review
- Every Sunday, review your tracking document
- Update any completed items
- Plan activities for the upcoming week

### Monthly Check
- First day of each month: Review last month's completion
- Plan upcoming gifts, letters, and activities
- Update journal entry about ways she expresses love

### Date Night Planning
- Schedule all 12 months at the beginning of the year
- Book babysitters at least 2 weeks in advance
- Make restaurant/activity reservations 1 week ahead

### Gift Ideas Bank
Keep a running list of gift ideas so you're never scrambling:
- Things she mentions wanting
- Items that remind you of her
- Experiences she'd enjoy
- Books, movies, music she's interested in

### Letter Writing Prompts
If you're stuck on what to write:
- Favorite memory from the past 3 weeks
- Qualities you admire in her
- Ways she's made your life better
- Dreams and goals for your future together
- Simple "I love you because..." list

### Goal Support Ideas
- Ask about her current goals and challenges
- Offer practical help (time, research, resources)
- Celebrate her wins, no matter how small
- Remove obstacles where you can
- Be her cheerleader and encourager

- For babysitters: Include contact info and rate

## Troubleshooting

### "No entries found in section"
- Check that section headers match exactly: `[GIFTS]`, `[LETTERS]`, etc.
- Ensure brackets are square brackets [ ], not parentheses ( )

### "Invalid date format"
- Use YYYY-MM-DD format only
- Example: 2025-10-24, not 10/24/2025 or Oct 24, 2025

### "System not finding calendar events"
- Use the recommended naming patterns above
- Check that calendar events have the correct calendar ID
- Verify timezone settings match your calendar

### "Toggl hours not tracking"
- Ensure project name matches exactly
- Check that project is in the correct workspace
- Verify API token has access to the project
